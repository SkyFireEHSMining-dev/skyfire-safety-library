Field Safety Resources

Safety resources, guidance, and field-proven insights from industry experts.

Powered by MSHA Safety Services Inc.

Training, documents, practical guidance, and expert-backed field support

This module is designed to make useful mine safety information easier to access in the field. The goal is to surface practical resources, trusted guidance, and field-proven insights that help crews make stronger decisions.

It is not meant to replace expert support. It is meant to increase access to helpful information and make it easier for users to recognize when they should reach out to a professional for deeper help.

Featured Articles

A space for DJ’s published safety articles, practical writeups, and field lessons that users can quickly browse inside the app.

Useful MSHA Documents

A quick-access section for free forms, checklists, training materials, and MSHA documents that crews can use without digging through websites.

Training FAQs

A field-friendly place for common training questions, practical answers, and reminders based on the kind of questions operators ask most often.

Ask MSHA AI

A future place for quick-answer guidance that helps users get pointed in the right direction while still encouraging them to reach out for expert advice when needed.

Standards / Best Practices / Advice

A section for field-proven guidance, operating norms, practical reminders, and expert advice that helps teams think beyond bare compliance.

Contact a Professional

A future contact area for direct support when the user needs help beyond what an in-app field reference should provide.
