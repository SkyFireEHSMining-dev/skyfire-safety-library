Report an Accident

Immediate actions and reporting reminders.

Immediate Actions

  1. Stabilize the scene and protect life first.
  2. Call emergency help if needed.
  3. Preserve critical evidence when safe to do so.
  4. Notify the proper chain of command.

Collect This Information

  • Who was involved
  • What happened
  • When it happened
  • Where it happened
  • Immediate hazards still present
